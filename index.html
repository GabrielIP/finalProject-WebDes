About (Click me)

This final project for the Information and Web Programming class is by Chris Johnson and Gabriel Pagliere
The app lets you create new items with the "New" button near the bottom.
After naming the new item, enter text in the box to the right.
The item is saved when you click save.
Utilizing local storage, these items persist upon browser closing.
Coming back to the site will reveal all your old data, unless you cleared the browser cache.
